Work Experience

Ph.D.

November 2013 - December 2017

Loria - University of Lorraine, Nancy, France

I was working in the research laboratory Loria, being a member of Madynes Team, under the supervision of Ye-Qiong Song and Bernardetta Addis. My work was funded by SATELOR project. SATELOR aimed to develop and sell a complete solution for elderly patients living alone in a complete sensorized environment, improving their health. The title of my thesis is “Multi-constrained QoS Routing and Energy Optimization for Wireless Sensor Networks”.

The goal of my thesis was to develop a routing protocol suitable for the low power wireless sensor networks, providing the required QoS. I proposed a probabilistic routing protocol, which increases the network lifetime through energy optimisation in MAC and Network layer. Moreover, I proposed a multi-constrained routing protocol, which takes into account multiple QoS metrics simultaneously, solving the multi-constrained path problem. I performed extended simulations in Contiki.
